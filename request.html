Emergency Blood Request

In many medical emergencies patients urgently require blood transfusions. Accident victims, surgery patients, and people suffering from severe illnesses may need blood immediately.

This page allows patients or their relatives to request blood quickly. Providing accurate information helps blood banks and donors respond faster.
